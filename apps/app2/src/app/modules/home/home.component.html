Welcome to the Voting Center

Take note of the following before you begin voting

  1. Don't rush anything but know that you have approximately two minutes to finish voting

  2. Log out by clicking the logout button at the top right corner of this page to finish and submit your voting data

  3. If you are not done or finished voting after the set time, you will be automatically logged out.

  4. Don't worry your data will be saved for you.

  5. Lastly, you cannot come back or log in again with your credential after logging out or being logged out.

Follow the instructions below to vote for your favorite candidate

  1. Start voting from the very begining

  2. On the card for your favorite candidate for the current position, click the

    vote button or either Yes / Nobuttons

  3. Click next to go to another position
